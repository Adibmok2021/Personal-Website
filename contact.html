About Me

My name is Muhammad Adib Hazim Bin Hj Norazli. I was born in Hospital Pertiwi,Petaling Jaya.

Hobby

My hobby is a passion for playing video games. From the moment I pick up a controller or sit at the keyboard, I am transported to a world of limitless possibilities and excitement. Whether it's exploring vast open landscapes, solving intricate puzzles, or engaging in intense multiplayer battles, video games offer me an immersive experience like no other. The thrill of overcoming challenges and the sense of accomplishment when I conquer a difficult level or achieve victory in a competitive match fuels my enthusiasm even further. Gaming has become an integral part of my life, providing not only entertainment but also opportunities to connect with like-minded individuals in online communities. It's a hobby that continues to bring me joy and endless hours of fun.
